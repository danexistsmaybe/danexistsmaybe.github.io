&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Don’t&nbsp;look.&nbsp;Listen.&nbsp;Listen&nbsp;closely.&nbsp;Listen&nbsp;for&nbsp;the&nbsp;splash&nbsp;of&nbsp;a&nbsp;pensive&nbsp;painted&nbsp;turtle.&nbsp;Listen&nbsp;for&nbsp;the&nbsp;fret-buzz-shriek&nbsp;of&nbsp;the&nbsp;cicadas&nbsp;that&nbsp;swells&nbsp;with&nbsp;their&nbsp;desire.&nbsp;Once&nbsp;their&nbsp;sound&nbsp;becomes&nbsp;pantheistic,&nbsp;merely&nbsp;a&nbsp;constant,&nbsp;unobtrusive&nbsp;presence,&nbsp;we,&nbsp;who&nbsp;are&nbsp;always&nbsp;hungry&nbsp;and&nbsp;looking&nbsp;for&nbsp;a&nbsp;little&nbsp;snack,&nbsp;could&nbsp;never&nbsp;find&nbsp;them.&nbsp;Sometimes&nbsp;I&nbsp;wonder&nbsp;if&nbsp;they&nbsp;play&nbsp;a&nbsp;game&nbsp;every&nbsp;evening&nbsp;at&nbsp;the&nbsp;break&nbsp;of&nbsp;dusk:&nbsp;who&nbsp;begins&nbsp;the&nbsp;cacophony?&nbsp;Who&nbsp;will&nbsp;be&nbsp;the&nbsp;first&nbsp;to&nbsp;let&nbsp;the&nbsp;others&nbsp;know&nbsp;it&nbsp;is&nbsp;okay&nbsp;to&nbsp;give&nbsp;in&nbsp;to&nbsp;the&nbsp;want,&nbsp;the&nbsp;need&nbsp;to&nbsp;not&nbsp;be&nbsp;quiet&nbsp;and&nbsp;alone?&nbsp;Who&nbsp;will&nbsp;risk&nbsp;it&nbsp;all?&nbsp;They&nbsp;are&nbsp;not&nbsp;very&nbsp;brave,&nbsp;these&nbsp;little&nbsp;ones.&nbsp;A&nbsp;desperate&nbsp;soul&nbsp;will&nbsp;break&nbsp;the&nbsp;silence;&nbsp;another&nbsp;will&nbsp;answer;&nbsp;then,&nbsp;nothing.&nbsp;A&nbsp;few&nbsp;minutes&nbsp;later&nbsp;it&nbsp;happens&nbsp;again.&nbsp;Eventually,&nbsp;they&nbsp;are&nbsp;all&nbsp;singing,&nbsp;but&nbsp;what&nbsp;listener&nbsp;could&nbsp;forget&nbsp;their&nbsp;haphazard,&nbsp;awkward&nbsp;beginning!&nbsp;So&nbsp;listen.&nbsp;Sometimes&nbsp;bridges&nbsp;look&nbsp;like&nbsp;barricades,&nbsp;but&nbsp;they&nbsp;are&nbsp;really&nbsp;only&nbsp;bridges.&nbsp;They&nbsp;carry&nbsp;you&nbsp;over&nbsp;the&nbsp;river,&nbsp;over&nbsp;your&nbsp;own&nbsp;reflection,&nbsp;and&nbsp;down&nbsp;a&nbsp;path&nbsp;with&nbsp;autumn&nbsp;maples—except,&nbsp;the&nbsp;air&nbsp;is&nbsp;night,&nbsp;so&nbsp;the&nbsp;gold&nbsp;has&nbsp;fallen&nbsp;off&nbsp;and&nbsp;left&nbsp;the&nbsp;most&nbsp;comforting&nbsp;kind&nbsp;of&nbsp;darkness&nbsp;to&nbsp;cloak&nbsp;each&nbsp;branch.&nbsp;Down,&nbsp;you&nbsp;will&nbsp;walk,&nbsp;under&nbsp;the&nbsp;infrastructure&nbsp;(because&nbsp;the&nbsp;under-infrastructure&nbsp;places&nbsp;are&nbsp;always&nbsp;nice&nbsp;places&nbsp;to&nbsp;be),&nbsp;and&nbsp;there&nbsp;you&nbsp;will&nbsp;find&nbsp;a&nbsp;flame.&nbsp;What&nbsp;kind&nbsp;of&nbsp;flame&nbsp;is&nbsp;it?&nbsp;It&nbsp;is&nbsp;the&nbsp;flame&nbsp;born&nbsp;on&nbsp;candles&nbsp;and&nbsp;lighters,&nbsp;the&nbsp;one&nbsp;that&nbsp;is&nbsp;soft&nbsp;and&nbsp;destined&nbsp;to&nbsp;go&nbsp;out,&nbsp;but&nbsp;will&nbsp;be&nbsp;beautiful&nbsp;while&nbsp;it&nbsp;burns.&nbsp;Except&nbsp;that&nbsp;this&nbsp;one&nbsp;is&nbsp;encased&nbsp;in&nbsp;crystal&nbsp;and&nbsp;covered&nbsp;in&nbsp;cobwebs.&nbsp;There&nbsp;are&nbsp;spiders&nbsp;too,&nbsp;some&nbsp;small&nbsp;and&nbsp;some&nbsp;plump,&nbsp;and&nbsp;I&nbsp;promise&nbsp;they&nbsp;would&nbsp;be&nbsp;cute&nbsp;if&nbsp;you&nbsp;could&nbsp;only&nbsp;see&nbsp;them&nbsp;in&nbsp;the&nbsp;daylight.&nbsp;Are&nbsp;you&nbsp;listening?&nbsp;Did&nbsp;you&nbsp;hear&nbsp;the&nbsp;words&nbsp;written&nbsp;above&nbsp;the&nbsp;light?&nbsp;Who&nbsp;wrote&nbsp;them&nbsp;there,&nbsp;inscribed&nbsp;on&nbsp;cement?&nbsp;I’m&nbsp;not&nbsp;sure.&nbsp;I&nbsp;only&nbsp;hope&nbsp;someone&nbsp;heard&nbsp;them.<br><br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;“First,&nbsp;I&nbsp;see&nbsp;the&nbsp;graffiti.&nbsp;Graffiti&nbsp;is&nbsp;a&nbsp;symbol&nbsp;of&nbsp;the&nbsp;human&nbsp;desire&nbsp;for&nbsp;expression.&nbsp;The&nbsp;seemingly&nbsp;meaningless&nbsp;words&nbsp;of&nbsp;graffiti&nbsp;are&nbsp;the&nbsp;language&nbsp;of&nbsp;a&nbsp;culture&nbsp;that&nbsp;runs&nbsp;against&nbsp;the&nbsp;norm,&nbsp;against&nbsp;capital,&nbsp;even.&nbsp;There&nbsp;is&nbsp;very&nbsp;little&nbsp;willing&nbsp;consumption&nbsp;of&nbsp;graffiti,&nbsp;and&nbsp;there&nbsp;is&nbsp;no&nbsp;industry.&nbsp;It&nbsp;is&nbs
